# Cloning of wild type lacI operators

Regulatory sequences were ordered from IDF (company information), containing a RNAP binding site followed by one of three repressor binding sites for lacI, both flanked by a primer pair.

|GCTTATTCGTGCCGTGTTAT|TCGAGTTTACACTTTATGCTTCCGGCTCGTATAATGTGTGG|AAATGTGAGCGAGTAACAACC|GGGCACAGCAATCAAAAGTA|
|GCTTATTCGTGCCGTGTTAT|TCGAGTTTACACTTTATGCTTCCGGCTCGTATAATGTGTGG|GGCAGTGAGCGCAACGCAATT|GGGCACAGCAATCAAAAGTA|
